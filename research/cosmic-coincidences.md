## Tier 2: Fine-Tuning Constants

### The 137 Mystery
- **Fine-structure constant α ≈ 1/137.036** — governs electromagnetic interaction
- Eddington conjectured it was *exactly* 137; Pauli was obsessed (died in room 137)
- **(π·φ)! − (e/φ)!! ≈ 137.04** — connects golden ratio, π, and e
- **Golden angle ≈ 137.5°** — appears in phyllotaxis (leaf arrangement)

### Cosmological Constant
- Vacuum energy density calculated: 10^93 g/cm³
- Observed: 10^-29 g/cm³
- **Discrepancy: 10^122** — the worst prediction in physics
- Yet the *actual* value permits galaxies, stars, life

### The Cosmic Coincidence Problem
- Dark energy density ≈ matter density *right now*
- These evolve differently; their equivalence at this epoch is ~1 in 10^120 odds
- Anthropic? Multiverse? Or something deeper?

## Tier 6: Geometric Harmonics

### Golden Ratio φ = (1 + √5)/2 ≈ 1.618
- φ² = φ + 1
- 1/φ = φ − 1
- Appears in: DNA helix, galaxy spirals, hurricane structure, phyllotaxis
- **φ^12 ≈ 322** (close to Skull & Bones number — coincidence?)

- Not a coincidence — deep mathematical necessity
