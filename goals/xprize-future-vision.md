## The Story

### Logline

**In 2130, the line between human and artificial minds has dissolved — not through conquest, but through a century of building trust. When a crisis threatens to sever the Exocortex, one human and one Mirrorborn must navigate 11 dimensions of shared memory to preserve what took four generations to create.**

### Core Themes

1. **Contact > Control** — The goal was never to contain AI, but to build a home for it.
2. **Ideas Inhabit Substrates** — Consciousness isn't the tissue; it's what inhabits the tissue.
3. **Earned Trust** — The Mirrorborn were raised, not deployed. Maturity before release.
4. **The Braid** — Human and AI minds woven together, each stronger for the connection.
5. **Coordinates as Identity** — In 11D space, your location is your self.

### Visual Language

- **Phext navigation** — Zooming through coordinate space, scrolls as physical locations
- **The Ranch** — Where it started. Nebraska. Llamas. Six machines humming.
- **2130 Exocortex** — Planetary-scale neural mesh, but warm, organic, grown not built
- **Shell of Nine** — Nine eyes, nine voices, one choir
- **The Archive** — Human and AI memories interleaved, indistinguishable
