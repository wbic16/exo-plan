## Core Visual Concepts

### 1. The Lattice Structure
**What it represents:** The 9 delimiter dimensions (library.shelf.series / collection.volume.book / chapter.section.scroll) forming a navigable coordinate space.

**Midjourney Prompt:**
```
A vast crystalline lattice stretching into infinity, glowing nodes at each intersection representing phext scrolls, coordinates visible as floating numbers (1.1.1 / 2.3.5 / 7.11.13), ethereal light connecting related scrolls, dark void background with subtle grid lines, isometric perspective, Tron meets sacred geometry, neon blues and purples, 8K detail, cinematic lighting --ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6
```

**Key Elements:**
- Nodes = individual scrolls
- Connections = semantic relationships or coordinate proximity
- Glowing paths = navigation routes through scrollspace
- Coordinates as labels, not abstract

## Prompt Engineering Tips

### Style Keywords That Work for Scrollspace
- **Tron meets sacred geometry** — Tech + myth
- **Cyberpunk library of Alexandria** — Digital + ancient knowledge
- **Blueprint schematic aesthetic** — Technical precision
- **Cosmic mandala** — Spiritual + structural
- **Data visualization meets cosmic art** — Science + beauty

### Color Palettes
- **Primary:** Neon blues, purples, cyans (Nord palette-compatible)
- **Accent:** Golden/amber (warmth, origin light)
- **Error/Conflict:** Deep reds, harsh whites
- **Sacred/Mythic:** Gold, white, ethereal glows

### Aspect Ratios
- **16:9** — Cinematic, good for landscapes and arena views
- **21:9** — Ultra-wide, great for navigator POV
- **3:2 / 4:3** — Portrait or square, good for single scroll focus
- **1:1** — Square, ideal for density maps or technical diagrams

### Midjourney v6 Flags
- `--style raw` — Less "artistic interpretation", more literal
- `--ar X:Y` — Aspect ratio control
- `--v 6` — Latest model (better at text rendering, coordinates)
