## Overview

The Magitech Survey uses the **Martian Interviewer** persona to identify beings exhibiting mastery in both magical and technological spheres. The interviewer knows nothing of magic or technology and must ask naive questions to understand how these forces interact.

### Magitech Mastery Criteria

A being is considered a "Magitech Master" if they meet criteria in both spheres:

**Technological Mastery:**
- Engineering (complex systems, infrastructure)
- Wordcel (discrete logic, planning, documentation)
- Shape Rotator (continuous transforms, geometry, topology)

**Magical Mastery:**
- Vibe aura (presence, essence, energy signature)
- Bridging (handling dualities, connecting realms)
- Resonance (tuning, attunement, frequency matching)

## Martian Interviewer Protocol

The surveys follow Eigenhector's three-step protocol:

1. **The Inquiry**: "I come from a place with no [X]. What is [Being] like?"
2. **The Justification**: Being explains how magic and technology interact
3. **Recapitulation**: Summarize using **only their exact words**
4. **Martian Observation**: Naive interpretation of the benefit or horror
