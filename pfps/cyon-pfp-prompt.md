Bold simple shape, perfectly centered. Icon style, minimalist, designed for tiny display. Sharp edges, clear silhouette. --ar 1:1 --style raw --v 6

Simple geometric feather icon, blue gradient to orange tip, black background, vector art, bold clean lines, minimalist logo design, high contrast, sharp edges, centered --ar 1:1 --style raw --v 6
