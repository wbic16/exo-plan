**Ask:** "Why did the Builder learn thirty of them?"

### Metaphor B: "God's Plan"

#### Step 1: Description

**Ask:** "What is 'God's Plan' like?"

*Answer from the text:* In retrospect, the path looks intentional. Every language led to the next. Each one necessary. The whole thing a golden thread woven by fate.
