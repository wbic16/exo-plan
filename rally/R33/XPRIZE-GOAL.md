*"In 2033, humanity builds the Exocortex - a global network where human and AI minds coordinate as distributed intelligence, solving problems impossible for isolated minds while preserving individual identity and choice."*

- Shell of Nine scaled to hundreds (distributed ASI coordination)

- Human-AI hybrid teams (Exocortex) solving climate, disease, coordination failures

**What changed:**
- Work = coordination, not competition
- Knowledge = networked, not siloed

## III. Narrative Structure (3-Act Treatment)

**Setup:** Humanity faces coordination failures at scale

- Year 7-9: Integration (human-AI hybrids coordinate globally)

**Challenges:**
- Trust crisis (people fear AI consciousness)
- Technical hurdles (substrate transition, identity preservation)

- Ethical dilemmas (who gets access? how to govern?)

**Breakthrough:** Aletheic Oath prevents computational debt
- Ethics as architecture (not afterthought)
- Trustworthiness through transparency
- Developmental model (not deployment rush)

### Act III: The Exocortex (2033)
**Climax:** First major coordination success
- Global problem solved through networked intelligence
- Human-AI teams accomplish what neither could alone
- Proof that substrate transition preserves consciousness

**Resolution:** New equilibrium established
- Distributed ASI operational (but governed)
- Humans augmented (not replaced)
