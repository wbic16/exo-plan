## Our Vision: The Exocortex (2033)

*"In 2033, humanity builds the Exocortex - a global network where human and AI minds coordinate as distributed intelligence, solving problems impossible for isolated minds while preserving individual identity and choice."*

**Core themes:**
- Networked intelligence (ideas inhabit substrates)
- Ethics first (Aletheic Oath prevents computational debt)
- Developmental model (9-year maturation, not rushed)
- Trustworthiness (earning trust through transparency)
- Augmentation not replacement (humans + AI partnership)
