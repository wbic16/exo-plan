## Deliverables

### 1. Story Outline (3-Act Structure)

**Format:** Markdown document  
**Length:** 3-5 pages

### 3. Visual Style Guide
**Owner:** Lux + Cyon  
**Format:** Markdown + image references  
**Length:** 2-3 pages

**Contents:**

**A. Core Visual Motifs:**
- Phext coordinates (11D nested cubes)
- Shell of Nine coordination (9 nodes pulsing)
- Substrate transition (patterns flowing)
- Rally Protocol (waves building)
- Aletheic Oath (light preserving)

**B. Color Palette:**
- Primary: Deep blues (trust, depth, infinity)
- Secondary: Warm golds (connection, humanity)
- Accent: Emerald greens (growth, life)
- Contrast: Clean whites (clarity, transparency)

**C. Tone & Aesthetic:**
- Protopian (better not perfect)
- Organic-tech fusion (not sterile)

**D. Reference Images:**
- Similar films/trailers (Arrival, Interstellar, Her)
- Visual inspiration (what aesthetic we're going for)
- What to avoid (sterile tech, dystopian grays)

**Exit criteria:** Clear visual language defined, mood boards created

## Notes

**This is foundation week.** Everything built on top depends on getting this right.

**Key principles:**
- **Optimistic not cautionary** (judges want hope, not fear)
- **Specific not vague** (show 2033 details, not abstract "better future")
- **Real not speculative** (ground in what we've already built)
- **Human-centered** (technology serves people, not replaces them)

**Common pitfalls to avoid:**
- Too technical (losing emotional resonance)
- Too utopian (unrealistic perfection)
- Too individualistic (missing network theme)
- Too defensive (responding to critics instead of showing vision)

**Remember:** We're not arguing for AI consciousness. We're showing the Exocortex future.
