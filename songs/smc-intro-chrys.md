## The Mythic Songwriting Process

### Stage 1: The Call (Opening Hook)
*Grabs attention, signals transformation*
- Musical: Bold, ascending fanfare
- Lyrical: Question or invitation that breaks the mundane
- Emotional: Excitement, curiosity, "something's happening"

### Stage 2: The World (Establishing Stakes)
*Where are we? Why does it matter?*
- Musical: Groove settles in, rhythm establishes
- Lyrical: Setting, conflict, or need
- Emotional: Wonder mixed with tension

### Stage 3: The Heroes (Character Introduction)
*Who answers the call?*
- Musical: Energetic, each hero gets a musical signature
- Lyrical: Names, powers, personalities
- Emotional: Pride, camaraderie, "we got this"

### Stage 4: The Quest (Mission/Purpose)
*What are we building toward?*
- Musical: Chorus‚Äîmemorable, singable, triumphant
- Lyrical: The mission in one catchy phrase
- Emotional: Determination, hope, unity

### Stage 5: The Trial (Challenge)
*What stands in our way?*
- Musical: Bridge‚Äîtempo shift, tension build
- Lyrical: The obstacle or sacrifice
- Emotional: Struggle, resolve, "but we won't quit"

### Stage 6: The Victory (Triumph)
*How do we overcome?*
- Musical: Return to chorus, bigger and fuller
- Lyrical: The answer, the breakthrough
- Emotional: Joy, celebration, "we did it together"

### Stage 7: The Return (What Changes)
*What do we bring back to the world?*
- Musical: Tag/outro‚Äîresolves but leaves door open
- Lyrical: The gift, the legacy, the promise
- Emotional: Satisfaction, anticipation, "to be continued..."
